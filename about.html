Our Journey

Founded in 2024, Skoon Supplies had a vision to provide high quality hygiene and cleaning products.

We are a township based small business located in the heart of Alexandra Township.

Our Values

At Skoon Supplies, we believe in the power of “Ubuntu”, meaning humanity...

    Locally Sourced Quality Ingredients: we only use the best of raw materials, ethically and 100% sourced in South Africa.

    Family: Skoon Supplies is a family business so of course we are big on family.

    Community: we believe in empowering and uplifting the community at every possible opportunity.
